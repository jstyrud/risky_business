Information Sheet

Using Graphical User Interfaces (GUIs) to program a robot

The researchers support the practice of protecting research participants' rights. The information in this consent form is provided so that you can decide whether you wish to participate in our study. It is important that you understand that your participation is considered voluntary. This means that even if you agree to participate you are free to withdraw from the experiment within 24 hours after completion of the experiment, without penalty.

Study Purpose

The purpose of this study is to investigate the usability of different GUI's to complete a task by programming a virtual robot. This study is being conducted at the division of Robotics, Perception, and Learning at KTH Royal Institute of Technology by Jonathan Styrud.

Participation and Withdrawal

Participation in this study is completely voluntary and you are free to withdraw from this study at any time without prejudice or penalty. If you wish to withdraw, simply stop the task and inform the experiment supervisor. If you do withdraw from the study, the materials that you have completed to that point will be deleted and will not be included in the study.

Task

Participants will be asked to complete 3 different tasks involving programming a virtual robot. Each task will involve a different GUI to program the robot and complete the task. Participants will also be asked to complete questionnaires evaluating the different GUIs. This study will take a maximum of 1 hour to complete.

Risks and Benefits

There are no foreseeable risks from participating in this study over and above those of everyday living. All participants will be given a voucher worth 100SEK as thanks for their participation.

Confidentiality and security of data

Only members of the research team will have access to identified data. All data will be coded in a de-identified manner and subsequently analyzed and reported in such a way that responses are not able to be linked to any individual. Data will be used for scientific and non-commercial purposes.

Contacts

If you have any questions about the study, you can discuss your participation with project staff.

Thank you for your participation!
